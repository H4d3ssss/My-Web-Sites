Sinigang ni Ralffie

Sinigang ni Ralffie isn’t just a dish—it’s a hug in a bowl! Sour, savory, and packed with all the good stuff, this recipe takes the classic Filipino sinigang and gives it Ralffie’s special touch. Imagine tender meat swimming in a tamarind broth that makes your face do that happy-asim reaction, with veggies that stay fresh and crunchy. It’s the kind of ulam that makes rice disappear faster than you realize. Whether it’s raining outside or you just need a taste of comfort, Sinigang ni Ralffie will always hit the spot—masarap, maasim, at punong-puno ng pagmamahal!

Ingredients

  • ½ kg pork belly (or shrimp/fish if you like)
  • 1 pack sinigang mix (or fresh tamarind)
  • 2 medium tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 bunch kangkong, trimmed

Instructions

  1. Boil the pork in water until tender.
  2. Add tomatoes and let them soften.
  3. Pour in the sinigang mix and simmer for a few minutes.
  4. Add kangkong, cook for 1–2 minutes, then serve hot with rice.
